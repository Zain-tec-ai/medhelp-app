Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

MedHelp provides educational health information, medicine salt label scanning, appointment requests, and contact support. This starter app currently stores demo form entries and saved topics on your device using browser local storage.

Information The App Handles

The app may handle names, phone numbers, email addresses, appointment reasons, medicine label images selected by the user, detected medicine label text, and in-app saved topics. If you connect a backend later, disclose exactly what is collected, stored, shared, and deleted.

Medicine Images And OCR

Medicine label images are selected by the user for salt detection. In this demo, OCR runs in the browser when the OCR library is available. Do not upload images to a server unless your production privacy policy clearly explains that use.

Health Disclaimer

MedHelp is not a medical diagnosis tool. It does not replace a doctor, pharmacist, or emergency service. Users should verify medicine salts, dose, safety, allergies, and interactions with a qualified professional.
